# Manage Shoot Budget

Keep estimate, committed cost and actual cost distinct.

## Categories

Creative/production fees; crew; talent and usage; location/studio; permits/insurance/security; camera/grip/lighting/digital; set/props/wardrobe/HMU; travel/accommodation/transport; catering/craft; post-production/delivery; taxes/service fees; contingency.

## Workflow

1. Record currency, tax treatment, client cap, included/excluded items and estimate version.
2. Give every line an owner, quantity, unit rate, days/hours, overtime assumption, status and source/quote.
3. Calculate subtotal, tax/fees and contingency with `calculate_shoot_budget`.
4. Track `estimated`, `committed` and `actual`; explain material variance.
5. Model base, lean and protected-quality scenarios. Do not cut legal, insurance or safety requirements to meet budget.
6. Set approval thresholds and purchase-order deadlines.

Never fabricate vendor quotes. Label allowances and estimates. Exclude sensitive bank/tax identifiers from general production documents.
