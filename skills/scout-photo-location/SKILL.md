# Scout Photo Location

Separate visual promise from operational reality.

## Workflow

1. Resolve exact coordinates and legal owner/manager.
2. Define required orientation, foreground, background, working distance, ceiling/space, sound sensitivity, power, load-in, privacy and accessibility.
3. Use astronomy/weather tools for light direction, golden/blue hour, moon/Milky Way, cloud layers, wind and precipitation when relevant.
4. Verify hours, gates, parking, restrooms, cell service, emergency access, crowd/noise patterns and travel time.
5. Identify permit, location agreement, insurance, drone, tripod, commercial-use, amplified-sound, generator and fire-safety requirements. Treat these as unverified until confirmed by the authority.
6. Score creative fit, light, logistics, access, cost, legal readiness, safety and weather resilience from 0–10 with evidence.
7. Select primary and backup locations; define the weather or access trigger and decision deadline.

## Scout report

Include coordinates; contact/authority; reference photos needed; sun/moon orientation; dimensions; load-in route; facilities; power; permit status; fees; restrictions; risks; backup; revisit questions. Never claim a location is permitted without written confirmation.
