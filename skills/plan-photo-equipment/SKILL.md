# Plan Photo Equipment

Derive gear from the shot list, location scout, crew capability and failure risk.

## Workflow

1. Map each setup to camera, lens/perspective, support, lighting, modifiers, grip, power, tether/data and audio/video needs.
2. Check location constraints: outlets, circuit capacity, cable runs, ceiling/footprint, wind, rain/dust, heat/cold, transport and permits.
3. Separate owned, rented, consumable and client-supplied items. Record quantity, pickup/return, owner and test status.
4. Add redundancy for single points of failure: body, media, batteries, trigger/sync, cables, power and data backup.
5. Use `recommend_camera_settings` only as a metering starting point.
6. Build prep-day tests and pack/load/unload checklists.

Include sandbags and safety cables where appropriate. Do not recommend overhead, electrical, generator or rigging work beyond qualified crew and local rules.
